1. Training of simple motor reaction

Training device:
• Ring - initial position and the end of the exercise.
• High tone signal - execute.

The simulator will model for you the situation in a fencing bout or during an individual lesson, when you need to respond to an action of your opponent (coach) with a simple conditioned premeditative action.

Exercise 1.

Imagine that you want to execute a direct attack on the preparation of your opponent.
Initial position: Middle distance (lunge distance), enguard position. You are constantly moving with one step forward - one step backwards.
With the signal execute a direct attack with lunge.

Exercise 2.

Imagine that you want to perform a quarte parry and riposte against the direct attack of your opponent to the inside sector.
Initial position: Close distance (direct hit distance), enguard position. You are bouncing on both feet.
With the signal perform one jump backwards, execute a direct parry quarte and the riposte with a lunge.

Exercise 3.

Imagine that you want to perform a circular six parry and riposte against the direct attack of your opponent to the inside sector.
Initial position: Close distance (direct hit distance), enguard position.
With the signal perform one step back, execute a circular parry sixth and riposte with a lunge.
